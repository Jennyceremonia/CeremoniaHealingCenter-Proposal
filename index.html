Our Mission & Shared Values

We begin with a heartfelt acknowledgment of your church's foundational role in this community. For years, you have been a place of sanctuary, guidance, and support, driven by core values of compassion, service to others, and the alleviation of suffering.

It is on this common ground that we wish to build. Our mission is to establish a licensed Natural Medicine Healing Center dedicated to providing safe, evidence-based, and deeply compassionate care for individuals navigating profound mental health challenges.

We aim to be a place where modern healing practices and timeless values of human dignity and wellness converge. Our goal is not just to treat symptoms, but to help people rediscover a sense of wholeness, hope, and connection to their lives, families, and community.

A Gentle Introduction to Our Approach

One of the core services we offer is psychedelic-assisted therapy. It is essential to understand that this is a structured, medically supervised therapeutic process, not recreational use. It is a catalyst for healing, comprised of three key phases:

1. Preparation

Multiple preparation sessions (consultations or coaching calls) to build trust and set intentions for the experience.

The Science of Healing & Safety

While the concept may seem new, it is grounded in decades of serious scientific research. Esteemed institutions such as Johns Hopkins University, Imperial College London, and MAPS have produced a growing body of evidence demonstrating remarkable efficacy for conditions like PTSD, severe anxiety, and depression.

The state of Colorado has recognized this potential by creating a regulated, licensed system for natural medicine healing centers. This ensures these therapies are delivered with the highest standards of safety and care, which is our utmost priority.

Our Unwavering Commitment

Your trust and the well-being of our clients are our paramount concerns. We want to be unequivocally clear about our commitment to operating with the highest level of integrity through four pillars of practice:

  • Professional Oversight: Our medical staff reviews each client's eligibility to approve them for the service. All sessions are then conducted by thoroughly vetted, state-licensed facilitators.
  • Rigorous Protocols: We follow strict, state-approved protocols for client screening, preparation, supervised sessions, and follow-up integration therapy.
  • Safe & Supportive Environment: Our center is designed to be a sanctuary—a calm, welcoming, and professional space conducive to healing and reflection.
  • Full Compliance: We will operate in strict adherence to every regulation set forth by the Colorado Department of Regulatory Agencies (DORA).
